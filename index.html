SecondVictor website is now live

17th May 2024

I am pleased to announce the launch of my website, I made this small site so that I can link to and also showcase projects I am currently working on, and hopefully document their ongoing development. I expected to have the site up in a day or two but building it took longer than I thought, as I decided to add web design to my ever evolving skillset. I did this by completing the fantastic web development course by Angela Yu. I'm sure that I redesigned the whole site at least a dozen times before settling on this really minimal theme and layout, Inspired in some part by John Grubers minimalist daringfireball.net.

I am planing to publish regular news about development updates to the software projects I am working on. The site may be pretty barebones for a short while so please be patient with me as I slowly add more content.

Thanks for visiting.

Allan.
