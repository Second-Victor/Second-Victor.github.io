About SecondVictor

My name is Allan Davis, and I am an independent iOS app developer with a passion for all things Apple..

I purchased the original iPhone in 2007 and was blown away as soon as I removed it from the box. It was unlike any consumer product released before. The device truly became powerful when Apple added the App Store in 2008. At that moment, I knew I had to somehow get an app on this tiny device and become a small part of its story. I decided to teach myself how to code in Objective-C during my spare time.
