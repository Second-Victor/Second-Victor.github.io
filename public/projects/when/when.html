When..

Keep track of all your events..

Know exactly when and where the things that matter to you took place..

When.. is an app for logging exactly when and where you were as an event in your life happens. This can be for something really mundane like what time did you feed the goldfish or even for important events like saving the details of a car accident location etc.

The app makes it easy to add an event and log your location. You also get the option to add notes or even multiple times and locations to the same event.

  • Save the exact time the event took place.
  • Store multiple times and locations per event.
  • Add notes.
  • Easily see the elapsed time.
  • 100% made with Swift and SwiftUI.
  • iOS exclusive.
